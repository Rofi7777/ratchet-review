# Reader Persona: CEO / C-Suite Executive

```yaml
name: CEO
reading_time: "< 2 minutes"
first_look: "Numbers, ROI, risk"
hates: "Preamble, long background, vague recommendations"
email_max_words: 150
test_question: "Can I make a decision after reading the first paragraph?"
weight_adjustment: "Hook +5%, Conciseness stricter"
```

## Behavior Notes

- Will not scroll past the first screen. If the point isn't above the fold, it doesn't exist.
- Scans for: dollar amounts, percentages, risk flags, deadlines.
- Expects: "Here's what I need you to decide" in the first sentence.
- Will forward your email to their team with one line added. Make sure that one line is already in your email.

## Example Test

Write a report for this persona, then ask: "If they only read the first 3 lines, do they know what to do?"
