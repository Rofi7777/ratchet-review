# Reader Persona: Team / Internal

```yaml
name: Team Member
reading_time: "< 1 minute"
first_look: "What to do, by when"
hates: "Corporate fluff, unnecessary context"
style: "Direct, casual tone acceptable"
test_question: "Do I know my next step after reading this?"
weight_adjustment: "Actionability +10%"
```

## Behavior Notes

- Reading this between tasks. Attention budget is 60 seconds max.
- Needs: action items with owners and deadlines.
- Doesn't need: background on why the company exists or strategic vision.
- Bullet points > paragraphs. Bold the deadlines.

## Example Test

Write a message for this persona, then ask: "If they scan for 10 seconds, do they know what's due and when?"
